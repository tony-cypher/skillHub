About Us

Welcome to Skill Hub We are not just another web application, we are your go-to platform for connecting with talented artisans across Nigeria. Created by cohort 2 fellows from LM Tech Hub and IG Tech Hub in Anambra State, Skill Hub was born from a simple idea to make it easier for people like you to find and hire skilled professionals to bring your projects to life.

What We Offer

  • Explore a diverse range of professionals right in your area, each one handpicked for their expertise and reliability.
  • With just a few clicks, you can reach out to artisans, share your needs, and get to know them better before deciding.
  • Read reviews from other users, check ratings, and view portfolios to ensure you choose the right person for the job.

Our Mission

At Skill Hub, we believe that great talent shouldn’t be hard to find. Our mission is to bridge the distance between you and the skilled artisans in your community. Whether you need a plumber to fix that pesky leak, an electrician to brighten up your home, or a tailor to create that perfect outfit, we are here to make those connections quick and easy.
